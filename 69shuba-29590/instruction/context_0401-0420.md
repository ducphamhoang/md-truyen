### NEW STORY CONTEXT
*   **City of Silver Incident (Chaps 400-403):** Derrick Berg (The Sun) exposes the contamination of his teammate Dack Rigins. He uses a ritual to sacrifice a contaminated item to The Fool, which also cleanses the Amon parasite within him. Chief Colin Iliad and Elder Lovia witness the ritual's aftermath. Lovia remains a controversial figure, suspected of being a 'Secrets Suppliant'.
*   **The Revelation of the Fool (Chaps 402-403):** The 'True Creator' sends a revelation to Mr. A of the Aurora Order to find 'The Fool' and his followers in Backlund. The honorific name is now known to the Aurora Order.
*   **Will Auceptin's "Death" (Chaps 404-406):** Klein and Dr. Aaron find the body of the boy Will Auceptin. Divination reveals Will has 'abandoned' his body to escape a rival 'Snake of Mercury' and is seeking a new form of survival (reincarnation).
*   **The Abraham Legacy (Chaps 405-409):** Lawrence (Abraham family) dies and leaves a notebook and a 'Traveler' characteristic to Fors, requesting she deliver them to Dorian Gray in Pritz Harbor. Dorian starts mentoring Fors via correspondence.
*   **The Desire Apostle Case (Chaps 413-419):** A high-sequence Beyonder of the Devil pathway, a 'Desire Apostle', begins hunting detectives who were involved in the Devil Dog case. Isengard Stanton and Casland are targeted. Klein teams up with them and the 'Machinery Heart' (using the artifact Arrodes) to fend off the killer.
