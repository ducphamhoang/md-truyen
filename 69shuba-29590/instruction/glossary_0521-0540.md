**Relationships**
* **Klein (as Gehrman) & Danitz:** Temporary alliance; Gehrman acts as a powerful, "mad" adventurer who has essentially captured/employed Danitz.
* **Danitz & Edwina Edwards:** Danitz is the boatswain (fourth officer) for "Vice Admiral Iceberg" on the ship *Golden Dream*.
* **Senor & Maveti:** Maveti is the second mate under "Admiral of Blood" Senor.
* **Audrey & Susie:** Audrey is the owner of Susie; both are Beyonders of the Spectator pathway and treat each other as partners.
* **Alger Wilson & Church of the Lord of Storms:** Alger is a captain/member of the Church (official Beyonder) but also a member of the Tarot Club.
* **The Tarot Club:** Klein (The Fool/The World), Audrey (Justice), and Alger (The Hanged Man) are secret associates.
