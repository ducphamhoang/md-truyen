### **NEW STORY CONTEXT (Chapters 301–320)**
*   **Sherlock Moriarty's Identity:** Klein has established himself as a reliable detective in Backlund, collaborating with **Isengard Stanton** and other detectives to hunt a **Serial Killer (Seq 7)** likely moving towards **Devil (Seq 6)**.
*   **The Harvest Church Incident:** Klein helps **Bishop Utrasky** (a former pirate from Frysac with Giant blood) purge his "inner demon" (his past persona as a **Dawn Knight**). In return, he receives the **Apothecary** formula and a **Master Key**.
*   **The Sanguine (Huyết Tộc):** Klein discovers a Sanguine named **Emlyn White** trapped in the Harvest Church's basement, forced to listen to sermons after being caught while "stealing" blood.
*   **Abyss Pathway Traits:** Klein learns that members of the **Abyss (Thâm Uyên)** pathway have a unique "danger intuition," allowing them to sense when someone intends to harm them within a certain timeframe (minutes to a day).
*   **Roselle's Legacy:** Klein scouts the **Kingdom Museum** during the Roselle Memorial Exhibition, identifying a potential **Blasphemy Card (Bia Báng Bổ)** disguised as a bookmark within Roselle's "Creative Manuscripts."
