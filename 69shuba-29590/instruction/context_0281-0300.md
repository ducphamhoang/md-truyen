# NEW STORY CONTEXT (Chapters 0281-0300)

### Major Events

**1. Sealing the Underground Ruins (Ch 281):**
Klein and bodyguard Sharon successfully collapse the Williams Street underground ruins using explosives, sealing the Evil Spirit inside. Klein returns the three Living Corpses safely and completes Miller Carter's commission. However, Klein is arrested by police while carrying a concealed weapon (residual gunpowder smell from practice), requiring lawyer Jurgen Cooper's intervention.

**2. The East Borough Serial Murder Case Intensifies:**
* Klein investigates the explosion site at **Dalavystreet** where **Lanevus** was last seen. Using the "All-Black Eye," he discovers a tall, wolf-like man hiding in the crime scene—a Sequence 6 or 5 **Shepherd (Mục Dương Nhân)** named **Steve**.
* Audrey (Justice) relays Steve's image to Hugh and Fors.
* Klein attends a Psychology Alchemists gathering disguised as a medical student and learns about the organization's structure.
* The serial killer strikes again during the **Blood Moon**, committing the 11th murder. Public panic intensifies.

**3. Introduction of The Magician - Fors Wall:**
* Fors suffers from a mysterious **Full Moon Curse**: every full moon (especially blood moons), she hears maddening whispers and sees terrifying visions of a door covered in patterns. The curse worsens each time.
* During a particularly severe Blood Moon episode, Fors is on the brink of losing control. Klein (The Fool) responds to Audrey's prayer and pulls Fors into the Gray Fog, saving her life.
* Klein deduces that the whispers come from **"Mr. Door"**, an ancient being sealed outside reality, possibly from the Fourth Epoch.
* Fors joins the Tarot Club and draws the **"Magician"** card as her codename.

**4. Klein's Advancement and New Abilities:**
* Klein successfully digests the "Clown" potion and is promoted to Sequence 7 **"Magician" (Ma Thuật Sư)**.
* New abilities include: **Paper Figurine Substitutes** (can create paper dolls to take fatal damage), **Flame Control**, **Air Bullets**, and enhanced illusions.
* Klein begins preparing for future advancement to Sequence 6 **"Faceless" (Vô Diện Nhân)**, which requires rare materials like **Mutated Pituitary of a Thousand-Faced Hunter**.

**5. Short-Term Missions:**
* Klein accepts a new bodyguard mission from **Loco Caroman**, a jewel merchant whose son **Yatru** is threatened by "bad friends."
* Klein visits a circus to observe magician performances and gain inspiration for "acting."

### NEW RELATIONSHIPS

* **Klein (The Fool) ↔ Fors Wall (The Magician)**: Savior and saved. Fors owes Klein her life and becomes a Tarot Club member. Klein is intrigued by the "Mr. Door" mystery connected to Fors.
* **Fors Wall ↔ Hugh Dilcha**: Close friends and roommates. Hugh is a Sequence 9 **Arbitrator (Trọng Tài Nhân)** who cares deeply for Fors and her curse.
* **Klein ↔ Steve (The Shepherd)**: Hunter and prey. Steve is connected to Lanevus and is a dangerous high-sequence Beyonder of the **Secrets Suppliant** pathway.
* **Klein ↔ Loco Caroman & Yatru**: New client relationship. The case may involve gang conflicts or Beyonder dangers.
