### NEW STORY CONTEXT (Chapters 251–260)
*   **The Ambassador's End**: The assassination of Ambassador Bakerland is carried out by Mr. A (the 'Shepherd'). Mr. A uses a combination of powers, including 'Rose Bishop' (Tường Vi Chủ Tế) to hide inside the vessel Irene, 'Corrupt Baron' to manipulate rules, and 'Devil' transformation. He eventually kills the Ambassador using a 'Record' (Ghi chép) of a high-level attack from the 'Recorder' (Ký Lục Quan) sequence.
*   **The Death of Rosago**: Klein successfully kills Rosago using the 'Dirty Word' charm to summon the True Creator's ravings. After the battle, Klein channels Rosago's spirit on the Gray Fog, obtaining the complete recipes for the 'Magician', 'Faceless', and 'Marionettist' sequences, as well as the ritual for Sequence 5.
*   **The 'Attraction' Theory**: Klein reflects on his journey and realizes he seems to 'attract' elements of the Seer pathway (Antigonus notebook, Sealed Artifact 2-049, Rosago). He suspects his connection to the Gray Fog creates a passive 'pull' for related Beyonder characteristics.
*   **The Serial Killer Case**: A brutal murder occurs in Backlund where a woman's internal organs are removed, bearing the hallmarks of the 'Criminal' (Tội Phạm) pathway and 'Blood Sanctum' (Bái Huyết Giáo) rituals.
*   **Williams Street Mystery**: A new client, Miller Carter, hires Sherlock Moriarty to investigate a hidden underground structure discovered during renovations of an old house on Williams Street, formerly owned by a bankrupt viscount.

### NEW RELATIONSHIPS
*   **Klein ↔ Miller Carter**: Professional. Miller Carter is an old-fashioned gentleman from Midseah who hires Klein to explore a potentially dangerous underground ruins without involving the authorities.
